30-Day Implementation Checklist

The 3-Shift Escape Plan - Your Day-by-Day Roadmap to Freedom

WEEK 1: Foundation

Set up your AI team and prepare for launch

1

Watch the Welcome Video 15 min

Get the complete overview of your escape plan.

  • Watch Module 1, Lesson 1
  • Take notes on the 3-shift concept
  • Set your 90-day goal
2

Set Up OpenClaw 30 min

Your AI command center starts here.

  • Create OpenClaw account
  • Install the app on your phone
  • Complete the onboarding tutorial
  • Set up your first agent
3

Configure ChatGPT 20 min

Your content creation engine.

  • Upgrade to ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
  • Create custom instructions for your niche
  • Save 3 starter prompts from the course
  • Generate your first piece of content
4

Set Up Image Creation 25 min

Visuals that stop the scroll.

  • Create Ideogram account (free)
  • Generate 5 brand images
  • Save your favorite prompts
  • Create a brand style guide
5

Choose Your Niche 30 min

What will you be known for?

  • Review the niche selection worksheet
  • List your skills and experiences
  • Research 3 potential niches
  • Make your final decision
6

Create Your Brand 45 min

Look professional from day one.

  • Design your logo (use AI or Canva)
  • Choose brand colors
  • Write your bio
  • Create social media handles
7

Week 1 Review 15 min

Celebrate your progress.

  • Review all completed tasks
  • Fix any setup issues
  • Plan Week 2 priorities
  • Take a break - you earned it!

WEEK 2: First Content

Create and publish your first pieces

8

Content Strategy 30 min

What will you post?

  • Choose your primary platform (TikTok, IG, LinkedIn)
  • Study 5 successful accounts in your niche
  • List 10 content ideas
  • Create content calendar template
9

Create Post #1 45 min

Your first piece of content.

  • Write hook (first 3 seconds)
  • Create main content body
  • Design visual/image
  • Add call-to-action
10

Publish Post #1 15 min

Hit publish - no perfectionism.

  • Post on your chosen platform
  • Use 5-7 relevant hashtags
  • Respond to first 3 comments
  • Screenshot for your records
11

Create Post #2 40 min

Build momentum.

  • Use AI to generate 3 variations
  • Pick the best one
  • Create visual
  • Schedule for tomorrow
12

Engagement Strategy 30 min

Build your community.

  • Find 10 accounts in your niche
  • Leave thoughtful comments on 5 posts
  • Reply to all comments on your posts
  • DM 2 potential collaborators
13

Create Post #3 40 min

Establish your rhythm.

  • Create value-packed content
  • Try a different format (carousel, video, text)
  • Post and promote
  • Analyze what worked from previous posts
14

Week 2 Review 20 min

Measure and adjust.

  • Check analytics on all posts
  • Note which content performed best
  • Plan next week's content
  • Celebrate publishing 3 posts!

WEEK 3: Monetization

Set up your first income stream

15

Choose Your Offer 45 min

What will you sell?

  • Review affiliate options in your niche
  • Sign up for 2-3 affiliate programs
  • Get your affiliate links
  • Test the products yourself
16

Create Lead Magnet 60 min

Give value to get emails.

  • Choose lead magnet topic
  • Create PDF using Canva or Google Docs
  • Design cover page
  • Export as PDF
17

Set Up Email Capture 45 min

Build your list.

  • Create AWeber or Mailchimp account
  • Set up landing page
  • Connect lead magnet delivery
  • Test the signup flow
18

Promote Your Lead Magnet 30 min

Get your first subscribers.

  • Create post about your free guide
  • Add link to your bio
  • Pin the post to your profile
  • Share in 2 relevant groups/communities
19

Create Soft Sell Content 45 min

Warm up your audience.

  • Share your personal story
  • Talk about the problem your product solves
  • Mention the solution without hard selling
  • Include subtle affiliate link
20

Direct Promotion 40 min

Make your first offer.

  • Create direct sales post
  • Include clear call-to-action
  • Add affiliate link
  • Post and monitor comments
21

Week 3 Review 20 min

Check your progress.

  • Count new email subscribers
  • Check affiliate dashboard for clicks
  • Review which content drove traffic
  • Adjust strategy for Week 4

WEEK 4: Scale

Double down on what works

22

Analyze Everything 45 min

Data tells the story.

  • Export analytics from all platforms
  • Identify top 3 performing posts
  • Note common elements in winners
  • Plan more content like your best performers
23

Repurpose Content 60 min

Work smarter, not harder.

  • Take your best post
  • Turn it into 3 different formats
  • Adapt for different platforms
  • Schedule for the week
24

Automate Workflows 45 min

Set up systems.

  • Create content templates
  • Set up scheduling tool (Buffer, Later)
  • Build email welcome sequence
  • Document your process
25

Expand Platforms 40 min

Multiply your reach.

  • Choose second platform to expand to
  • Adapt 5 pieces of content
  • Set up profile on new platform
  • Post your first piece there
26

Networking 45 min

Who you know matters.

  • Join 3 communities in your niche
  • Introduce yourself
  • Provide value in discussions
  • Connect with 5 peers
27

Plan Month 2 60 min

Think bigger.

  • Set income goal for Month 2
  • Plan content calendar
  • Identify new affiliate opportunities
  • Consider creating your own product
28

Celebrate Wins 30 min

You've come far.

  • List everything you've accomplished
  • Screenshot your analytics
  • Share your journey in a post
  • Treat yourself - you earned it!
29

System Optimization 45 min

Make it sustainable.

  • Review all your systems
  • Identify time-wasters
  • Automate repetitive tasks
  • Create weekly schedule template
30

Day 30: The New You 60 min

You're not the same person who started.

  • Write reflection on your transformation
  • Calculate total progress (followers, emails, sales)
  • Set 90-day goals
  • Commit to continuing the journey
  • YOU DID IT! 🎉
