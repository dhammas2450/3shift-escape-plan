DM Scripts That Convert

Word-for-Word Messages to Turn Conversations into Customers

How to Use These Scripts

1. Personalize: Replace the [bracketed text] with specific details about the person you're messaging.

2. Be Genuine: These are starting points - add your personality and voice.

3. Follow Up: Most sales happen after the 3rd-5th touch. Don't give up after one message.

4. Track: Note which scripts work best for your audience and refine over time.

Cold Outreach Scripts (1-10)

1

The Value-First Approach

When: Reaching out to someone in your niche who doesn't know you yet

Hey [Name]! 👋 I just saw your post about [specific topic] - really resonated with me, especially the part about [specific detail]. I've been working on something similar and discovered a [quick tip/resource] that's been game-changing. Thought you might find it helpful too. [Share tip or link] No pitch, just wanted to connect! Keep up the great work. [Your name]
Why it works: Provides immediate value without asking for anything. Builds goodwill first.
2

The Common Ground

When: You share a mutual connection or interest

Hi [Name]! I noticed we both follow [mutual connection] and I loved your recent post about [topic]. I'm also working in [niche] and have been trying to [specific goal]. Would love to connect with others on the same journey. What got you started in this space? [Your name]
Why it works: Mutual connections create instant trust. Asking a question invites response.
3

The Compliment + Question

When: You genuinely admire their work

[Name], I have to say - your [specific content/project] is incredible! The way you [specific detail you liked] really stood out. Most people miss that. Quick question: How did you approach [specific challenge]? I'm working through something similar and would love your perspective. [Your name]
Why it works: Specific compliments show you actually paid attention. People love talking about their expertise.
4

The Direct Value Proposition

When: You have a clear solution to their visible problem

Hey [Name], I saw your post about struggling with [specific problem]. I've been there! I actually help [target audience] solve exactly that using [your method]. Just helped someone go from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]. Not sure if it's a fit for you, but happy to share what worked if you're interested? [Your name]
Why it works: Addresses a pain point they're already vocal about. Soft pitch, easy out.
5

The Content Response

When: Reacting to their story, reel, or post

Just watched your story about [topic] - [genuine reaction]! I've been thinking about [related topic] a lot lately. Have you found that [ask for their insight]? Would love to hear your thoughts! [Your name]
Why it works: Timely and relevant to what they're currently thinking about.
6

The Curiosity Gap

When: You want them to ask YOU for more info

Hey [Name]! I've been following your journey with [their project/goal] - really impressive progress. I think I found something that could help you [specific benefit] even faster. Not sure if you're open to new strategies right now? [Your name]
Why it works: Creates curiosity. They have to respond to find out what you know.
7

The Referral Request

When: You want them to introduce you to someone

Hi [Name], Hope you're doing well! I saw you're connected to [target person's name]. I'm working on [brief description] and think it could really help them with [specific benefit]. Would you be comfortable making an intro? No worries at all if not! [Your name]
Why it works: Low pressure, respects their relationship. Easy to say no.
8

The Event Follow-Up

When: You met at an event or webinar

Hey [Name]! Great meeting you at [event name] yesterday. Really enjoyed our conversation about [topic you discussed]. You mentioned you were working on [their project] - would love to hear how that's going! [Your name] [Your company/role]
Why it works: Establishes existing connection. References specific conversation.
9

The Beta Tester Invite

When: Launching something new and want early adopters

Hey [Name]! I've been following your work in [niche] and really respect your insights. I'm about to launch [product/service] and looking for 5 people to beta test it at no cost in exchange for honest feedback. Given your experience with [relevant experience], you'd be perfect. Interested? [Your name]
Why it works: Exclusive opportunity. Positions them as expert. Free value first.
10

The Collaboration Pitch

When: You want to partner on content or project

Hi [Name], I've been thinking about creating content around [topic] and your perspective on [their angle] would be amazing. Would you be open to a quick collaboration? Maybe a [IG Live/podcast interview/joint post]? I think our audiences would love it. No pressure if timing doesn't work! [Your name]
Why it works: Mutual benefit. Shows you've thought about value for them.

Warm Lead Scripts (11-20)

11

The Check-In

When: Following up with someone who showed interest before

Hey [Name]! Just wanted to check in - last we talked you were dealing with [their situation]. How's that going? Still thinking about [your solution] or has the situation changed? [Your name]
Why it works: Shows you remember and care. Non-pushy follow-up.
12

The Value Follow-Up

When: They didn't respond to your first message

Hi [Name], Not sure if you saw my last message, but I came across this [article/tool/resource] and immediately thought of you. [Brief explanation of why it's relevant] Hope you find it helpful! [Your name]
Why it works: Doesn't mention the ignored message. Provides new value.
13

The Social Proof Nudge

When: They expressed interest but haven't committed

Hey [Name]! Quick update - just helped another [their type of business/person] achieve [specific result] using [your method]. Reminded me of our conversation. Still interested in exploring this for yourself? [Your name]
Why it works: FOMO. Shows results without being pushy.
14

The Urgency Creator

When: You have a legitimate deadline or limited spots

Hi [Name], Wanted to give you a heads up - I'm closing enrollment for [program/service] on [date] to focus on the current group. I know you were considering it. Want to grab one of the last spots or should I take you off the list? No pressure either way! [Your name]
Why it works: Creates real urgency. Gives them an easy out.
15

The Objection Handler

When: They said "I need to think about it"

Hey [Name], Totally understand wanting to think it over. Big decisions deserve consideration. Just to help with that - what specific questions or concerns are you weighing? Happy to clarify anything or provide more info that might help. [Your name]
Why it works: Gets them to voice real objections so you can address them.
16

The Price Conversation

When: They said "It's too expensive"

Hi [Name], I hear you on the investment. Let me ask - what's the cost of NOT solving [their problem]? If we could get you to [desired outcome] in [timeframe], what would that be worth? Happy to discuss payment options if that helps. What would work better for your budget? [Your name]
Why it works: Reframes cost vs value. Opens negotiation.
17

The "Not Right Now" Response

When: Timing isn't right for them

Hey [Name], No problem at all - timing is everything! When WOULD be a better time for you? I can check back then so you don't have to keep it on your mental to-do list. Also happy to add you to my [newsletter/content] in the meantime so you get value while you wait. [Your name]
Why it works: Gets specific timeline. Keeps them in your world.
18

The Spouse/Partner Objection

When: They need to check with someone else

Hi [Name], Makes total sense to discuss with [spouse/partner]. Want me to send you a quick summary of what we discussed that you can share with them? Might make the conversation easier. Also happy to hop on a quick call with both of you if they have questions! [Your name]
Why it works: Helps them sell it to their partner. Offers to talk to both.
19

The Competitor Mention

When: They're considering other options

Hey [Name], Smart to compare options! I always recommend doing your due diligence. A few questions to help you decide: - Does the other option include [your unique feature]? - What's their track record with [specific result]? - Do they offer [your guarantee/support]? Happy to chat through the differences if helpful! [Your name]
Why it works: Positions you as confident. Highlights your unique value.
20

The Final Follow-Up

When: Last attempt after multiple follow-ups

Hi [Name], I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back. Totally get it - you're busy and this might not be a priority right now. Going to assume it's a "not right now" and stop following up so I don't become annoying! 😊 If things change or you want to chat in the future, you know where to find me. No hard feelings either way. Wishing you all the best! [Your name]
Why it works: Takes pressure off. Often gets a response because they feel bad!

Closing Scripts (21-30)

21

The Assumptive Close

When: They've shown strong interest

Awesome [Name]! Sounds like this is exactly what you need. Ready to get started? I just need [payment info/contract signature] and we can begin [next step]. [Your name]
Why it works: Assumes the sale. Confident energy is contagious.
22

The Choice Close

When: They need to make a decision

Great talking with you [Name]! Based on what you shared, would you prefer: Option A: [Basic package] - gets you started with [core benefit] Option B: [Premium package] - includes everything in A plus [extra benefits] Which feels like a better fit for where you're at? [Your name]
Why it works: Either choice is a sale. Removes "yes/no" binary.
23

The Trial Close

When: Testing if they're ready to buy

Hey [Name], If we could get you to [desired outcome] by [date], would you be ready to move forward? Just want to make sure we're on the same page before I send over the details. [Your name]
Why it works: Gets commitment before presenting offer. Qualifies them.
24

The Summary Close

When: Recapping value before asking for sale

Perfect [Name]! So to confirm - you'll be getting: ✓ [Benefit 1] ✓ [Benefit 2] ✓ [Benefit 3] ✓ [Bonus] All for [price]. Ready to get started? I can send the payment link now. [Your name]
Why it works: Reminds them of full value. Makes saying yes easy.
25

The Urgency Close

When: There's a real deadline

Hi [Name], Just a heads up - the [bonus/discount/spot] expires tonight at midnight. I know you were interested. Want to grab it before it goes away? Here's the link: [payment link] [Your name]
Why it works: Real urgency motivates action. Simple and direct.
26

The Payment Plan Offer

When: Price is the final objection

Hey [Name], I want to make this work for you. What if we split it into [number] payments of [amount] instead? Same total, just easier on cash flow. Would that help? [Your name]
Why it works: Removes financial barrier. Shows flexibility.
27

The Guarantee Close

When: They're worried about risk

I totally understand wanting to be sure [Name]. Here's what I'll do - try it for [time period]. If you don't see [promised result], I'll refund every penny. No questions, no hassle. Sound fair? [Your name]
Why it works: Removes risk. Shows confidence in your offer.
28

The Scarcity Close

When: Limited spots available

Quick update [Name] - Only [number] spots left for this cohort. I've had [number] inquiries today. Want me to hold one for you? I can give you [time] to decide, then I'll need to release it to the next person. [Your name]
Why it works: Real scarcity creates FOMO. Limited-time hold adds pressure.
29

The Takeaway Close

When: They're being indecisive

Hey [Name], I get the sense this might not be the right time for you. And that's totally okay! I only want people who are 100% ready to commit. If you're not feeling it, no hard feelings at all. Want me to check back in a few months?
