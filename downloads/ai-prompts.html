50 AI Prompts

Copy-Paste Templates for Content, Sales & Automation

How to Use These Prompts

Simply copy any prompt below and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or your favorite AI tool. Replace the [bracketed text] with your specific information. These prompts are optimized to get high-quality output that you can use immediately.

Content Creation (Prompts 1-15)

1

Viral Hook Generator

Write 10 attention-grabbing hooks for a social media post about [TOPIC]. Each hook should be under 15 words and create curiosity or urgency. Use power words and emotional triggers.
Use for: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
2

Complete Post Writer

Write a [PLATFORM] post about [TOPIC] that is [TONE: funny/educational/inspirational]. Include: 1) Hook in first line, 2) 3 valuable points, 3) Call-to-action. Keep it under [WORD COUNT] words.
Use for: Any social platform
3

Carousel Post Creator

Create a 5-slide carousel post about [TOPIC]. For each slide: 1) Title (max 5 words), 2) Body text (max 30 words), 3) Visual description. Make it educational and shareable.
Use for: Instagram, LinkedIn
4

Video Script Writer

Write a 60-second video script about [TOPIC]. Format: Hook (0-3 sec), Problem (3-15 sec), Solution (15-45 sec), CTA (45-60 sec). Include visual cues in [brackets].
Use for: TikTok, Reels, Shorts
5

Blog Post Outline

Create a detailed outline for a blog post titled "[TITLE]". Include: Introduction hook, 5 main sections with subpoints, conclusion with CTA. Aim for 1500 words total.
Use for: SEO blog content
6

Email Subject Lines

Write 10 email subject lines for an email about [TOPIC]. Mix: curiosity, urgency, benefit-driven, and question formats. Keep under 50 characters each.
Use for: Email marketing
7

Newsletter Creator

Write a weekly newsletter about [NICHE]. Include: 1) Personal story/opening (100 words), 2) 3 curated tips/resources, 3) One actionable takeaway, 4) P.S. with soft pitch. Friendly, conversational tone.
Use for: Email newsletters
8

Quote Image Generator

Create 10 motivational quotes about [TOPIC] that would work well as Instagram posts. Each should be original, thought-provoking, and under 15 words.
Use for: Quote graphics, inspiration content
9

Myth Buster Post

Write a "Myth vs Truth" post about [TOPIC]. List 5 common myths and debunk each one with facts. Make it shareable and educational.
Use for: Educational content, authority building
10

Before/After Story

Write a compelling before/after transformation story about [TOPIC]. Structure: The struggle (emotional), the turning point, the transformation (specific results), the lesson. 200 words.
Use for: Testimonials, case studies
11

List Post Creator

Create a "Top 10" list about [TOPIC]. Each item should have: Number, Bold title (5 words max), 2-sentence explanation, Pro tip. Make it skimmable with emojis.
Use for: Viral list posts
12

FAQ Generator

Generate 10 frequently asked questions about [PRODUCT/SERVICE/TOPIC] along with detailed, helpful answers. Address objections and include a soft CTA in 3 of the answers.
Use for: FAQ pages, sales content
13

Controversial Opinion Post

Write a post sharing an unpopular opinion about [TOPIC]. Make it thought-provoking but respectful. Include: The controversial statement, Why you believe it, Evidence, Invitation for discussion.
Use for: Engagement, sparking discussion
14

Step-by-Step Tutorial

Create a step-by-step tutorial for [TASK]. Break it into 7 simple steps. Each step: Action title, Detailed instructions, Time estimate. Include a "What you'll need" list at the top.
Use for: How-to content, tutorials
15

Content Calendar Generator

Create a 7-day content calendar for [NICHE]. For each day: Content type, Topic, Hook idea, Call-to-action. Mix educational, entertaining, and promotional content.
Use for: Weekly planning

Sales & Copywriting (Prompts 16-30)

16

Sales Page Headlines

Write 10 headlines for a sales page selling [PRODUCT]. Mix: Benefit-driven, curiosity-based, pain-point focused, and results-oriented. Make them compelling and specific.
Use for: Landing pages, sales pages
17

Product Description

Write a product description for [PRODUCT] that sells. Include: Hook, Problem it solves, Features → Benefits, Social proof mention, Guarantee, Strong CTA. 150 words.
Use for: E-commerce, product pages
18

Email Sales Sequence

Write a 5-email sales sequence for [PRODUCT]. Email 1: Value/Story, Email 2: Problem agitation, Email 3: Solution introduction, Email 4: Objection handling, Email 5: Urgency/Scarcity. Include subject lines.
Use for: Email funnels
19

DM Sales Script

Write a DM conversation script for selling [PRODUCT]. Include: Opening (no pitch), Qualification questions, Value demonstration, Soft pitch, Objection responses, Closing. Natural, conversational tone.
Use for: Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger
20

Testimonial Request

Write a message asking a customer for a testimonial about [PRODUCT]. Make it easy for them by suggesting specific questions they could answer. Include a small incentive offer.
Use for: Getting social proof
21

Objection Handler

Write responses to these 5 common objections about [PRODUCT]: 1) Too expensive, 2) Need to think about it, 3) Don't have time, 4) Tried similar before, 5) Not right now. Empathetic but persuasive.
Use for: Sales calls, DMs, emails
22

Limited Time Offer

Create urgency copy for a [DISCOUNT]% off sale on [PRODUCT]. Include: Why now, What they get, Deadline, Scarcity element (if applicable), Risk reversal. Make it exciting but not pushy.
Use for: Flash sales, promotions
23

Webinar Script Opening

Write the first 10 minutes of a webinar about [TOPIC]. Include: Hook, Credibility establishment, Promise of value, Agenda, Engagement question. Energy should be high and captivating.
Use for: Webinars, live streams
24

Upsell Offer

Write an upsell offer for customers who bought [PRODUCT]. The upsell is [UPSELL PRODUCT]. Position it as the natural next step. Include: Logical connection, Added value, Special price, Guarantee.
Use for: Order bumps, upsells
25

Abandoned Cart Email

Write 3 abandoned cart emails for [PRODUCT]. Email 1 (1 hour): Friendly reminder. Email 2 (24 hours): Address concerns + small discount. Email 3 (72 hours): Last chance + social proof. Each under 100 words.
Use for: E-commerce recovery
26

About Page Bio

Write an About page for [YOUR NAME/BUSINESS]. Structure: Hook/Story opener, Struggle/transformation, Why you do what you do, Credibility markers, Personal touch, CTA. 300 words, conversational.
Use for: Website About page
27

Case Study Writer

Write a case study about how [CUSTOMER] achieved [RESULT] using [PRODUCT]. Include: Background, Challenge, Solution (your product), Implementation, Results (specific numbers), Testimonial quote. Professional but readable.
Use for: B2B sales, high-ticket offers
28

Referral Request

Write a message asking happy customers to refer friends to [PRODUCT]. Make it easy with suggested text they can copy/paste. Include: Gratitude, Clear ask, Incentive (if applicable), Simple instructions.
Use for: Referral programs
29

Price Justification

Write copy that justifies the price of [$X] for [PRODUCT]. Break down the value, compare to alternatives, show ROI, include guarantee. Make the price feel like a bargain.
Use for: High-ticket sales pages
30

Launch Announcement

Write a launch announcement for [PRODUCT]. Build excitement: Tease the problem, Hint at the solution, Build anticipation, Reveal with fanfare, Early bird special, Urgency. Multi-platform format.
Use for: Product launches

Automation & Systems (Prompts 31-40)

31

Email Welcome Sequence

Write a 3-email welcome sequence for new subscribers in [NICHE]. Email 1: Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself. Email 2: Share your story + valuable tip. Email 3: Soft pitch your main offer. Friendly, helpful tone.
Use for: Email automation
32

Content Repurposing Plan

Take this piece of content: [PASTE CONTENT]. Show me how to repurpose it into: 1) 5 social posts, 2) Email newsletter, 3) Blog excerpt, 4) Video script. Maximize reach from one piece.
Use for: Content efficiency
33

Standard Operating Procedure

Write an SOP for [TASK]. Include: Purpose, Tools needed, Step-by-step instructions (numbered), Time estimate, Quality checkpoints, Common mistakes to avoid. Clear enough for anyone to follow.
Use for: Delegation, training
34

Customer Support Response

Write templates for 5 common customer support scenarios: 1) Technical issue, 2) Refund request, 3) Feature question, 4) Complaint, 5) Praise/compliment. Empathetic, helpful, on-brand.
Use for: Customer service
35

Meeting Agenda

Create an agenda for a [DURATION] meeting about [TOPIC]. Include: Objective, 3-5 discussion items with time allocations, Decision points, Action items section. Professional format.
Use for: Team meetings, client calls
36

Project Brief

Write a project brief for [PROJECT]. Include: Objective, Target audience, Deliverables, Timeline, Budget, Success metrics, Stakeholders. Clear and comprehensive.
Use for: Freelancers, agencies
37

Weekly Review Template

Create a weekly business review template. Sections: Wins (3), Challenges (2), Metrics (key numbers), Lessons learned, Next week priorities (3), Resource needs. Quick to complete.
Use for: Business tracking
38

Onboarding Sequence

Write a 5-message onboarding sequence for new customers of [PRODUCT]. Messages: 1) Welcome + quick win, 2) Setup help, 3) Best practices, 4) Advanced tip, 5) Feedback request. Reduce churn.
Use for: Customer success
39

Outreach Template

Write a cold outreach message to [PROSPECT TYPE] about [OFFER]. Structure: Personalized opener, Value proposition, Social proof, Soft ask, Easy next step. Not salesy, conversational.
Use for: Partnerships, B2B sales
40

Survey Questions

Create a 5-question customer survey for [PRODUCT]. Questions should uncover: Satisfaction level, Favorite feature, Biggest pain point, Improvement suggestions, Likelihood to recommend. Mix question types.
Use for: Market research, feedback

Strategy & Planning (Prompts 41-50)

41

90-Day Goal Planner

Help me create a 90-day plan for [GOAL]. Break into: Month 1 (foundation), Month 2 (growth), Month 3 (scale). Include weekly milestones, key metrics, potential obstacles, and resources needed.
Use for: Quarterly planning
42

Competitor Analysis

Analyze [COMPETITOR] for me. Include: Their positioning, Strengths, Weaknesses, Pricing strategy, Content strategy, Gaps we can exploit. Actionable insights, not just observations.
Use for: Market research
43

Ideal Customer Profile

Create a detailed ideal customer profile for [PRODUCT]. Include: Demographics, Psychographics, Pain points, Goals, Objections, Where they hang out online, What they read/watch. Specific and vivid.
Use for: Targeting, messaging
44

SWOT Analysis

Conduct a SWOT analysis for [BUSINESS/PRODUCT]. Strengths (4), Weaknesses (4), Opportunities (4), Threats (4). Be honest and specific. Include action items for each.
Use for: Strategic planning
45

Pricing Strategy

Help me price [PRODUCT]. Consider: Costs, Competitor pricing, Perceived value, Target market, Payment options. Suggest 3 pricing tiers with features for each. Justify recommendations.
Use for: Product pricing
46

Content Audit

I need to audit my content about [NICHE]. Help me: Categorize existing content, Identify gaps, Find repurposing opportunities, Suggest content to retire, Plan new priorities. Data-driven approach.
Use for: Content strategy
47

Partnership Proposal

Write a partnership proposal to [POTENTIAL PARTNER]. Include: Why them specifically, Mutual benefit, Your audience/assets, Proposed collaboration, Next steps. Professional but warm.
Use for: Business development
48

Crisis Response Plan

Create a crisis response plan for [BUSINESS]. Include: Potential scenarios, Response team roles, Communication templates, Timeline for response, Follow-up actions. Be prepared for worst-case.
Use for: Risk management
49

Exit Interview Questions

Create exit interview questions for customers who cancel [PRODUCT]. Uncover: Real reason for leaving, What would have kept them, Feedback on product, Likelihood to return. Learn from churn.
Use for: Retention improvement
50

Annual Review Template

Create a comprehensive annual business review template. Sections: Year in review, Financial performance, Product/service performance, Marketing results, Team/operations, Customer feedback, Lessons learned, Next year goals (with metrics).
Use for: Yearly planning
