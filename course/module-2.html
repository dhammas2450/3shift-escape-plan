2 The 3 Types of Content That Sell

Not all content is created equal. Some content entertains. Some content educates. But only three types actually make you money.

Master these three. Ignore everything else (for now).

🎓

Educational Content

Teaches your audience something valuable. Builds trust. Positions you as the expert. Examples: How-to guides, tutorials, tips, tricks.

📖

Story-Based Content

Shares real experiences. Creates connection. Makes you relatable. Examples: Your journey, client wins, lessons learned, behind-the-scenes.

🏆

Proof Content

Shows results and builds credibility. Removes doubt. Examples: Case studies, testimonials, before/after, reviews, data.

🎯 Here's What This Means For You

Every week, you should create at least one of each type. That's your content formula. Educational builds authority. Stories build connection. Proof builds trust. Together, they build sales.

3 Using AI to Create 30 Days of Content in 1 Hour

Here's where it gets exciting. You don't need to stare at a blank screen anymore. AI can do the heavy lifting.

But here's the secret: You still need to be the brain. AI is your assistant, not your replacement.

The 1-Hour Content System

  1. Brain Dump (10 minutes)
    Write down 10 topics your audience cares about. Don't overthink. Just list problems they have and solutions you know.
  2. AI Expansion (15 minutes)
    Use ChatGPT or Claude. Give it this prompt: "I'm a [your role] helping [your audience]. Give me 3 social media posts about [topic] including hook, body, and call-to-action."
  3. Batch Create (20 minutes)
    Use Canva or similar. Create templates for your content types. Drop in the AI-generated text. Customize with your branding.
  4. Add Your Voice (10 minutes)
    Read through everything. Add personal touches. Change words to how YOU talk. This step is crucial — this is what makes it YOURS.
  5. Schedule Everything (5 minutes)
    Load it into your scheduling tool. Set it and forget it. Your month is now handled.

4 The Content Multiplication System

This is the secret weapon. This is how you get 10x results from every piece of content you create.

🌳 The Tree Analogy

Most people plant a seed, grow one tree, and get a few apples. Smart content creators plant one seed, grow a tree, then take cuttings to grow an entire orchard.

One piece of "pillar content" becomes 20+ smaller pieces. Work once. Multiply forever.

How It Works

Start With ONE Pillar Piece

This is your big content. A 10-minute video. A 2000-word blog post. A detailed podcast episode. Something substantial.

Then break it down:

  • Video → 5 short clips for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
  • Blog post → 10 social media quote cards
  • Podcast → 3 email newsletters
  • Any content → 1 week's worth of tweets/threads
  • Key points → LinkedIn article or carousel

5 Scheduling and Automation

Here's a truth that might sting: If you're posting manually, you're doing it wrong.

Manual posting is inconsistent. Life gets busy. You forget. Days turn into weeks. Your audience forgets you exist.

The "Set It and Forget It" System

  1. Pick Your Tool
    Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, or Meta Business Suite. They all do the same job. Pick one. Learn it well.
  2. Create Content Buckets
    Educational posts on Mondays. Stories on Wednesdays. Proof content on Fridays. Mix in promotional posts 20% of the time.
  3. Batch Upload
    Load your month's content in one sitting. Schedule for optimal times (typically 9am, 12pm, 6pm in your audience's timezone).
  4. Let It Run
    Your content goes live automatically. You show up consistently without thinking about it every day.
