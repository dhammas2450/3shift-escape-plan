🔨 Month 1: BUILD

DAYS 1-30

Build Your Foundation

Before you can sell anything, you need tools. You need content. You need something to offer. Month 1 is about building these pieces. No selling yet. Just building.
  • 1

    Set Up Your AI Tools

    Get your AI team ready. Set up OpenClaw for automation. Configure your HeyGen avatar. Connect your PostStream for social media. These are your workers. Get them hired and trained in week 1.

  • 2

    Create Your Core Content

    Make 10 pieces of content. Videos, posts, emails—whatever fits your style. These prove you know your stuff. They build trust. They attract people to you. Batch create them in week 2.

  • 3

    Build Your First Offer

    What will you sell? A course? Coaching? A done-for-you service? Pick ONE thing. Price it at $97, $297, or $997. Create a simple sales page. Week 3 is offer time.

  • 4

    Set Up Your Systems

    Connect your payment processor. Set up your email list. Build your delivery system (how customers get what they bought). Test everything. Make sure it works. Week 4 is system week.

💡 Pro Tip: Don't make your offer complicated. The best offers solve ONE problem for ONE person. "I help busy moms lose 10 pounds in 30 days" beats "I help everyone with everything" every time.

🚀 Month 2: LAUNCH

DAYS 31-60

Get Traffic, Get Leads, Get Sales

You built it. Now sell it. Month 2 is about getting eyeballs on your offer and turning those eyeballs into dollars. This is where the fun starts.
  • 1

    Start Posting Every Day

    Use your AI tools to post content daily. One video. One email. Three social posts. Minimum. This keeps you visible. This builds your audience. Week 5 is go-time.

  • 2

    Run Your First Ads (Optional)

    If you have $5-10/day, run simple Facebook or TikTok ads. Point them to a free guide or video. Collect emails. This speeds everything up. Week 6 if you want to accelerate.

  • 3

    Start Conversations

    Reply to every comment. Answer every DM. Jump in conversations where your target audience hangs out. Be helpful. Be real. Don't pitch—just connect. Week 7 is conversation week.

  • 4

    Make Your First Sale

    By day 60, you should have at least ONE paying customer. Celebrate it. Screenshot it. This proves your business works. One sale becomes ten. Ten becomes one hundred. Week 8 is sales week.

📊 Month 3: SCALE

DAYS 61-90

Systems, Automation & Growth

You proved it works. Now make it work WITHOUT you. Month 3 is about automation, systems, and scaling what already works. This is how you buy back your time.
  • 1

    Automate Your Content

    Use your AI tools to batch-create a month of content in one day. Schedule it all. Set it and forget it. Your content machine runs while you sleep. Week 9 is automation week.

  • 2

    Build Email Sequences

    Write 5-10 emails that sell for you. Set them to send automatically when someone joins your list. These emails work 24/7. They nurture. They sell. They never sleep. Week 10 is sequence week.

  • 3

    Double Down on What Works

    Look at your numbers. What content got the most views? What offer sold best? Do MORE of that. Stop doing what didn't work. Focus your energy. Week 11 is optimization week.

  • 4

    Add an Upsell or Second Offer

    Now that you have buyers, give them something else to buy. A higher-priced option. A done-with-you service. A bundle. More offers = more revenue per customer. Week 12 is expansion week.
