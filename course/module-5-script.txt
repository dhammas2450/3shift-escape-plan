[0:00-0:10] HOOK
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

"That's not living. That's surviving."

[Text overlay: "Tired of surviving?"]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

"90 days from now, you could be free."

"Free from the alarm clock. Free from the commute. Free from asking permission to live your life."

[Text overlay: "90 Days to Freedom"]

[0:50-1:20] THE 90-DAY OVERVIEW
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Let me break this down simple. Three months. Three phases."

"Month One: BUILD. You set up your tools. You create your content. You build your first offer. You're getting ready."

"Month Two: LAUNCH. You start posting every day. You get traffic. You get leads. You make your FIRST sale. This is where it gets real."

"Month Three: SCALE. You automate everything. You build systems. You add more offers. Your business starts running WITHOUT you."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I call it the 'Walmart Exit.' Here's how it works."

"When your business makes one and a half times your current paycheck—for THREE months straight—you can quit."

"That's it. That's the number."

"If you make $3,000 a month at your job, when your business makes $4,500 for three months, you're done. You walk in, you quit, you never look back."

"Not someday. Not when you're 65. NOW."

[Text overlay: "Walmart Exit = 1.5x Your Salary x 3 Months"]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"And here's the secret weapon. You don't need employees. You don't need an office. You need AI."

"Your Content AI writes for you. Your Video AI makes videos while you sleep. Your Social AI posts everywhere. Your Chat AI answers questions."

"You become the CEO of a robot workforce. They work 24/7. They never complain. They cost pennies compared to a human team."

"This isn't the future. This is RIGHT NOW."

[Text overlay: "6 AI Assistants = Your Dream Team"]

[2:20-2:50] THE NEW LIFE VISION
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Picture this."

[Pause - let them imagine]

"You wake up naturally. No alarm. You make coffee. You check your phone—sales came in overnight. While you were sleeping."

"You decide to work from a coffee shop today. Or the beach. Or don't work at all. It's YOUR choice."

"You pick up your kids from school. You have lunch with your spouse. You take Tuesday off because you feel like it."

"That's not a fantasy. That's what this business gives you. Time AND money."

[Text overlay: "Time + Money = True Freedom"]

[2:50-3:15] THE TRACK RECORD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"I've seen it happen. Over and over."

"The single mom who hit $5,000 a month and quit her night shift. The teacher who built a $10,000 a month business in summers off. The warehouse worker who now works from his patio."

"They all started exactly where you are. They just took action."

"The only difference between them and everyone else? They started."

[Text overlay: "Real People. Real Results."]

[3:15-3:40] THE 24-HOUR CHALLENGE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"So here's my challenge to you. You have 24 hours."

"Not next week. Not 'when you're ready.' 24 hours."

"Pick your start date. Write it down. Circle it. Commit to it."

"Post ONE piece of content. Doesn't have to be perfect. Just has to be DONE."

"Tell someone your plan. Say it out loud. 'I'm starting my 90-day escape plan.' Make it real."

[Text overlay: "24 Hours. 3 Actions. Start NOW."]

[3:40-4:10] THE URGENCY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Look, I need to be real with you. This opportunity won't last forever."

"AI is changing everything. The people who learn this NOW will be ahead for years. The people who wait? They'll be playing catch-up forever."

"The window is open. But it won't stay open."

"You have two choices. You can keep doing what you're doing. Keep waking up to alarms. Keep asking permission to live. Keep dreaming about 'someday.'"

"Or you can start TODAY."

[Text overlay: "The Window Is Open"]

[4:10-4:30] THE CALL TO ACTION
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"90 days. That's all I'm asking for. Follow the plan. Do the work. Watch your life change."

"Your freedom is waiting. Your new life is waiting."

"The only question is: Are you going to show up and claim it?"

"I hope you do. I'll see you on the inside."

"Let's go build your escape plan."
