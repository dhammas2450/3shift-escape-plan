================================================================================
MODULE 1 VIDEO SCRIPT - "THE FOUNDATION"
Duration: 3-4 minutes | Style: Chad Nicely Teaching Style
Avatar: Professional, enthusiastic, direct-to-camera
================================================================================

---
OPENING HOOK (First 10 Seconds - CRITICAL!)
---

[LOOK DIRECTLY AT CAMERA - ENERGY HIGH]

"What if I told you that for forty dollars a month... you could have a full-time team working for you twenty-four seven?

Not employees. Not contractors. AI.

And in the next three minutes, I'm going to show you exactly how to build it."

[PAUSE - LET THAT LAND]

---
SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)
---

"Most people are trapped.

They trade eight hours for eight hours of pay. Then they wake up and do it again. And again. For forty years.

Sound familiar?

Here's the truth that changed everything for me... and thousands of my students:

You don't need more time. You need a team.

But hiring people is expensive. Complicated. Risky.

So what do you do?"

---
SECTION 2: THE AI TEAM CONCEPT (45 seconds)
---

[LEAN IN - CONVERSATIONAL]

"You build an AI team.

Think of it like this. You just became a CEO. And your employees? They never sleep. Never call in sick. Never complain.

Your AI team has five members.

The Writer. Creates your content. Emails. Posts. Scripts.

The Designer. Makes everything look professional.

The Assistant. Answers questions. Organizes your day.

The Analyst. Crunches numbers. Spots opportunities.

And the Builder. Writes code. Creates automations.

Here's what this means for you... Instead of doing everything yourself, you become the director. You point. They execute."

---
SECTION 3: THE WALMART STORY (60 seconds)
---

[PAUSE - SHIFT TO STORY MODE]

"Let me tell you about my story.

I worked at Walmart. Day shifts. Ten AM to seven PM. I made twenty-four hundred dollars a month.

I was exhausted every day. Working full time while trying to build a business. Something had to change.

That was my breaking point.

I built my AI team in three shifts. Ninety days later? My side business made thirty-four hundred dollars in one month.

I put in my notice at Walmart. I work from home now. Building my dream every day.

I'm not a tech genius. I didn't go to college for this. I spent 40 years in carpentry. I just became the boss of my AI team.

If I can do it after 40 years in carpentry and working at Walmart... you can do it too."

---
SECTION 4: THE TOOLS (45 seconds)
---

[HOLD UP FINGERS - COUNT THEM OFF]

"You need four tools. That's it.

First... ChatGPT. Your AI assistant. Free version works great. Writes content. Answers questions. This is where you start.

Second... OpenClaw. Twenty bucks a month. Your automation engine. Works while you sleep.

Third... Canva. Thirteen bucks a month. Your designer. Drag and drop. No skills needed.

Fourth... HeyGen. Twenty-four bucks a month. Your video spokesperson. Creates content without you being on camera.

Total cost? Thirty-seven dollars a month.

For the price of one dinner out, you get a full team working around the clock."

---
SECTION 5: YOUR FIRST WIN (30 seconds)
---

[ENERGY UP - EXCITED]

"Here's your mission. Right now. Today.

Go to Kimi dot com. Create your free account. Takes five minutes.

Then ask it to do ONE thing for you.

Write your business bio. Create five social posts. Draft an email.

When you see AI create something useful in ten seconds... everything changes.

You realize you don't need to be creative. Or a good writer. You just need to ask.

That's your first win. And wins build momentum."

---
CLOSING CALL TO ACTION (20 seconds)
---

[LOOK DIRECTLY AT CAMERA - SERIOUS BUT ENCOURAGING]

"Listen. You have three choices.

One... Keep doing what you're doing. Trading time for money. Hoping something changes.

Two... Do nothing with this information. Let it be another thing you "meant to try."

Or three... Take one small action right now. Set up Kimi. Get your first win. Start building.

The only difference between trapped and free... is three shifts of setup.

Module Two is waiting. But only if you finish Module One.

Go build your team. I'll see you in the next video."

[POINT AT CAMERA]

"You've got this."

[FADE OUT]
